
### Full description (≤4000)
```
AsenaPlug is a privacy-first VPN that routes your traffic through a secure tunnel over
Cloudflare's MASQUE (WARP) infrastructure. The goal is simple: reach the open internet
without giving up speed — and without collecting any data that identifies you.

PRIVACY, BY DESIGN
• No logs, no tracking, no browsing history.
• No name, email or phone required — your account is anonymous.
• On first launch an anonymous WARP key is created on your device and stays only there.
  Your site list is never sent to a server; it stays on your phone.
• No ads, no analytics, no tracking SDKs.

FAST AND MODERN
• Cloudflare's global MASQUE (HTTP/2 & HTTP/3) backbone — low latency.
• "Everything" mode: all traffic tunneled. "Selected sites" mode: only the domains you
  choose go through the tunnel, everything else connects directly.
• Optional live speed test — tunnel speed when connected, direct speed when off.
• Persistent connection: an optional battery-optimization exemption keeps the tunnel
  alive in the background (only if you allow it).

CLEAN AND SLEEK
• Turkish and English; follows your system language.
• Light/dark theme, dynamic color accent.
• One-tap connect — a short guided tour on first launch.

HOW IT WORKS
Your traffic is carried over Cloudflare's MASQUE (connect-ip) protocol through an
encrypted tunnel. Cloudflare's handling of connection data is governed by its own
privacy policy; no data is sent to any other third party.

Privacy Policy: https://kaanalper.github.io/AsenaPlug/privacy.html
Contact: kaanalperkaraaslan@gmail.com

AsenaPlug — an open internet, privately.
```
