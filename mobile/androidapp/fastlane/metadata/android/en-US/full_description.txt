AsenaPlug is a privacy-first VPN that routes your traffic through a secure tunnel over Cloudflare's MASQUE (WARP) infrastructure. Reach a fast, secure and open internet without collecting any data that identifies you. No ads, no tracking, no clutter.

✓ No logs, no tracking, no browsing history

✓ Your anonymous key and site list stay only on your phone, never on a server
✓ No ads, no analytics, no tracking SDKs

⚡ FAST AND MODERN
✓ Cloudflare's global MASQUE (HTTP/2 & HTTP/3) backbone — low latency
✓ Smooth, stable connection
✓ Optional live speed test: tunnel speed when on, direct speed when off
✓ Stays alive in the background — the tunnel won't drop unless you want it to

🎯 TWO MODES, YOUR CHOICE
✓ Everything: all your traffic goes through the secure tunnel
✓ Selected sites: only the domains you pick are tunneled; everything else stays direct and full-speed

🎨 CLEAN AND SLEEK

✓ Light/dark theme, dynamic color accent

🛡️ HOW IT WORKS
Your traffic is carried over Cloudflare's MASQUE (connect-ip) protocol through an encrypted tunnel. Cloudflare's handling of connection data is governed by its own privacy policy; no data is sent to any other third party.
